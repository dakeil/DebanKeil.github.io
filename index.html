About Me

I'm Currently an Inventory auditor for a family owned business in the DFW area. Through my current job as an auditor, and my previous one as a supply chain specialist, I found that I have a knack for data. Cleaning, analyzing, and reporting on the results from my data were core parts of both my previous and current job. With the combined 7 years of supply chain and auditing experience, I have a great starting base as a data analyst.

Last year I started my journey to becoming a data analyst. I joined a data analytics boot camp with Merit America to jumpstart my intoduction to being a data analyst. Through Merit America I learned how to have a good mindset to be a succesful analyst as well as start learning technical skills with queary languages and visualization programs.
